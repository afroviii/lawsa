THE LAWSA SENATE

The Senate currently on it's 5th Sitting is made up of 20 senators, four from each constistuency excluding the members of the 500L constituency. The leadership of the Senate is presided by the Senate President and the Deputy Senate President. The other presiding officers of the Senate include the Majority Leader, the Minority Leader and A Sergeant of Arms.

The Senators are elected through a simple majority vote system and the elections are open to only members of the particular constituency. Another election to elect the presiding officers is held during a plenary session and requires obtaining a two-third majority.

The LAWSA Senate is governed pursuant to the provisions of the LAWSA Constitution. The Senate is the faculty's third highest Legislature the other two being the Staff Adviser and the Dean of the faculty.
